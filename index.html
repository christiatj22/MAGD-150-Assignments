Space Invaders 2015

Description: This sketch is based off the classic game Space Invaders. The enemies are the circular "space worms" slithering down to attack the space ship. Use W,A,S,D keys to move ship. Press mouse to change states between normal space and alert level.



Orbit

Description: This sketch is based off the theme of SciFi. There is the Sun with the Moon orbiting in front. Stars populate randomly. Move mouse to add UFOs on-screen.

Rectangle of Life

Description: This sketch is based off the theme of nature. There are 4 images of animals, one in each quadrant of the screen. When the mouse is moved to this quadrant, the animal will be displayed. The Lion King's "Circle of Life" song appropriately plays in the background.
